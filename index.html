Meta Tic-Tac-Toe Rules

Win three sub-boards in a row to win the game.

  • You play in the sub-board dictated by your opponent's last move.
  • If sent to a won or full board, you may choose any board.
  • The game ends when someone wins three boards in a row, or all boards are filled.
